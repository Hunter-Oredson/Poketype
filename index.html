About this website

This site will calculate which Pokémon type is super effective and not very effective to fight against.


To begin, just click on either the "Attacking" or "Defending" button located on the Menu. If you click "Attacking", this will refer to the type of Pokémon you are attacking, that means it could be multi-valued (two at most). The "Defending" button is for choosing what type of Pokémon you are defending with and will let you know what type of moves to look out for.

The "Generation" drop down in the menu will let you choose which generation of Pokémon you are referring to. You will notice that the older generations are missing some types of Pokémon compared to the newest generation.
